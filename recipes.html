Chicken Thigh BBQ Marinate (Pan seared)


3-4 cloves garlic
6 grinds of black pepper
3 TbSp soy sauce datu puti
1 TbSp vinegar
1 TeaSp salt
1 Half cup water
3 TbSp sugar
3 TbSp kalamansi

Instructions


Its best to do this in a stainless steel pot in my experience. Heat pot to medium-high (searing heat) and place Chicken skin into pot and sear the skin. It will bring the chicken oil out and rend the fat. Use this to cook the chicken I leave the pot lid on to have it broil and cook more evenly. Don't forget to lower the heat after the fats are gone!

Chinese Adobo


5 lbs pork (belly, sliced)
5 cloves garlic2 TbSp sugar
3 TbSp soy sauce
3 TbSp sugar
8 pcs peppercorns
1 Tsp salt and vinegar
2 pcs star anise

Cook sliced pork with salt and pepper (huge pot). Render fat and brown both sides of meat. Remove from pot. Put garlic, star anise, sugar on oil and let sugar brown. Add meat and stir, add vinegar and soy sauce and 1 cup water. Low heat until meat is tender.


Papa's spaghetti sauce


No quantities given just instructions! Mince garlic and onion, afterwards sautee in pan with olive oil. Add in the ground beef and cook until brown/gray. Add in salt, pepper, and thyme. Afterwards a can of tomato paste, diced/stewed canned tomatoes and finally red wine and sugar to top it off. Adjust flavoring with salt.


New York Cheesecake


Crust:
Honey Maid crackers x2 packs
Sugar
Unsalted butter

Filling
4 Eggs
4 packs of philadelphia cream cheese
1 serving of flavored or greek yogurt
2 cups of sugar
1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

General Instructions:
Make sure you let all the refrigerated ingredients hit room temperature before starting. It helps with mixing. For the crust, mix sugar first
and then drizzle melted butter whilst mixing so as to prevent clumping. Preheat oven to 375F and make sure to prepare a boiling water bath
before putting the mold inside.
