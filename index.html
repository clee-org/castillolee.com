Chinese Adobo

5 lbs pork (belly, sliced)
5 cloves garlic2 TbSp sugar
3 TbSp soy sauce
3 TbSp sugar
8 pcs peppercorns
1 Tsp salt and vinegar
2 pcs star anise


Cook sliced pork with salt and pepper (huge pot). Render fat and brown both sides of meat.
Remove from pot. Put garlic, star anise, sugar on oil and let sugar brown. Add meat and stir, add vinegar and soy sauce and 1 cup water. Low heat until meat is tender.
